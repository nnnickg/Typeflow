## Embedded Data Sources

- OPUS OpenSubtitles2018 monolingual text data, distributed by OPUS:
  https://opus.nlpl.eu/OpenSubtitles.php
- Frequency word lists from `hermitdave/FrequencyWords`:
  https://github.com/hermitdave/FrequencyWords

The OpenSubtitles page asks users of the corpus to link to
https://www.opensubtitles.org/ and cite:

> P. Lison and J. Tiedemann, 2016, OpenSubtitles2016: Extracting Large Parallel
> Corpora from Movie and TV Subtitles. In Proceedings of the 10th International
> Conference on Language Resources and Evaluation (LREC 2016).

`hermitdave/FrequencyWords` states that its code is MIT licensed and its
generated content is `CC-BY-SA-4.0`.

## Generated Artifacts

The checked-in artifacts are generated by `typeflow-data`:

- `crates/typeflow-core/data/en.ngrams.bin`
- `crates/typeflow-core/data/uk.ngrams.bin`
- `crates/typeflow-core/data/en.dict.fst`
- `crates/typeflow-core/data/uk.dict.fst`

They contain character n-gram probabilities and dictionary word-frequency maps,
not raw subtitle files. Rebuild them with:

```sh
cargo run --release -p typeflow-data
```
